Cal-ITP Conference

The California Integrated Travel Conference was held May 1st and 2nd 2018 and was the first major opportunity for CalSTA and its partners to present the concept of a statewide integrated travel planning and payment solution to an audience including many California public transit operators and transportation professionals; to invite open discussion on benefits, challenges, and best practices; and to solicit questions and feedback from all participants.
